# Ma nguon MS-DOS v1.25 va v2.0
Repo nay chua ma nguon goc va cac tep nhi phan da bien dich cho MS-DOS v1.25 va MS-DOS v2.0.

Chung la cac tep giong nhau [duoc chia se tu Bao Tang Lich Su May Tinh vao thang 3 ngay 25 nam 2014]( http://www.computerhistory.org/atchm/microsoft-ms-dos-early-source-code/) va dang duoc tai xuat ban trong repo nay de giup ho de dang tim thay, tham khao bang van ban va cong trinh ben ngoai va cho phep kham pha va thu nghiem cho nhung nguoi quan tam den He dieu hanh PC dau tien.  

# Giay phep
Tat ca cac tep trong repo này duoc phat hanh theo giay phep [MIT (OSI)]( https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/MIT_License) theo tep [Ban quyen] (https://github.com/Microsoft/MS-DOS/blob/master/LICENSE.md) duoc luu tru trong thu muc goc cua repo nay.
